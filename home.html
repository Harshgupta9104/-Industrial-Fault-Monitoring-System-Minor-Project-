About the Project

The Industrial Fault Monitoring System uses a network of intelligent sensors to detect real-time anomalies in environmental conditions.

DHT22 Sensor

Measures temperature and humidity to monitor ambient environment and detect overheating risks.

MQ135 Gas Sensor

Detects toxic gases such as CO₂, NH₃, benzene—essential for air quality and chemical detection.

PIR Motion Sensor

Senses movement in restricted or sensitive industrial areas to improve intrusion detection.

Flame Sensor

Detects fire or flame in hazardous zones for early fire suppression activation.

Alert System

Triggers buzzer, LED and mobile alerts to notify in case of fault detection.

NodeMCU + Blynk

Real-time data visualization and control through cloud using Blynk and MQTT integration.
